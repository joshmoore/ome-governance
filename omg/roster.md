# **OME Management Group — Roster**

## **1. Overview**

The OME Management Group (OMG) is the steering body of the OME ecosystem,
responsible for the creation and oversight of all subprojects. This document
records the current roster of OMG members as well as the process for modifying
this document.

## **3. Roles and Responsibilities**

- Oversight of Registered Projects and shared accounts as specified in the charter.
- Management of the Governance repository including this document
- Tie-breaking and other advisory duties for subprojects.

## **4. Document Maintenance**

This roster is maintained by the OMG and updated as needed to reflect changes
in participation, roles, and project evolution. Membership changes shall be
made in accordance with the decision-making procedures defined by the OMG
Charter. Unless otherwise specified, appointment or removal of OMG members
requires unanimous consent of the remaining OMG members, subject to any
conflict-of-interest or recusal provisions defined elsewhere.
